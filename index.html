Hi, I'm Songyan Yu, a quantitative hydro-ecologist. My research interests include hydrological modelling, surface water variation, environmental flow management, river health assessment, and systematic conservation prioritisation. I am currently working as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Australian Rivers Institute, Griffith University.

I completed my PhD in 2019 where I investigated the spatio-temporal dynamics and hydro-ecology of intermittent streams in eastern Australia. My focus was on quantifying the spatio-temporal patterns of flow intermittency, and development and application of a predictive model for surface water extent to identify aquatic habitat refuges.
