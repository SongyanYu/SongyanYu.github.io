Songyan's website

I submitted my PhD thesis in May 2019 and now am working as a post-doc research fellow at Australian Rivers Institute, Griffith University.

My research investigated the hydro-ecology and functions of intermittent streams in Australia. My focus was on quantifying spatial and temporal patterns of flow intermittency, and development and application of a predictive model for surface water extent to identify aquatic habitat refuges.

I was also working on hydrological modelling with particular interests in low flow simulations.

I completed his Master of Engineering degree in the field of environmental baseflow estimation and management at Beijing Normal University, China. Before that he undertook a Bachelor of Engineering degree at Wuhan University, China.
